MMU x Nexagate CTF 2023 Challenges

by ssi51

During my internship at Nexagate, I had the incredible opportunity to immerse myself by actively participating in the MMU x Nexagate CTF competition. One of the highlights of my experience was the creation of three captivating challenges within the Miscellaneous category. The experience not only honed my technical abilities but also allowed me to contribute meaningfully to the cybersecurity community. It was a privilege to be part of such an engaging and educational event while interning at Nexagate Sdn Bhd.

As part of my involvement in the MMU CTF competition, I created three challenges under the Miscellaneous category. Feel free to give them a try:

Uncover the Secret Headquarters

You stumble upon an article that claims to have uncovered the address of a secret headquarters. As you eagerly click on the link, you're greeted with an image of a bustling city street. Find the address of the exact location where this image was.

Flag format: NxMMU{StreetName}

Sushi Sleuth

Username "tbgmike" gave a 5 star review on a sushi place in Vancouver Canada. He claimed that the restaurant served well-prepared and fresh sushi. Can you find the name of the restaurant?

Flag format: NxMMU{NameOfRestaurant}

The Enigmatic Letter

Mabel, with her usual curiosity piqued, stumbled upon a dusty old letter hidden within the depths of the Mystery Shack's cluttered closet. Its worn parchment and fading ink hinted at its age, suggesting a long-lost secret waiting to be unraveled. Can you help her decode the message?

Flag format: NxMMU{MESSAGEHERE}
